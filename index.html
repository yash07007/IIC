About PDPU IIC

PDPU Innovation & Incubation Centre (PDPU IIC) is an incubator established by Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University (PDPU). PDPU IIC is registered u/s 8 of Companies Act, 2013. It is recognized as Nodal Institute by Govt. of Gujarat and registered as Startup India Incubator under DIPP (Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion, Govt. of India). Also, PDPU IIC is supported by Student Start-up and Innovation Policy(SSIP) by Education Dept. of Govt. of Gujarat, Startup Innovation Policy by Industries Commissionerate(IC) and Department of Science and Technology(DST) by Government of Gujarat. PDPU IIC is born with the aim of converting the brimming potential of budding Engineers, Management Students and Technocrats into innovation-driven business ventures leading to technical renaissance. We are incubating 50+ Start-ups from the different domains such as waste management, energy and environment, IT & IOT, Fintech, Cleantech, etc.


About PDPU

Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University (PDPU) which has a NAAC accreditation with "A" Grade, with CGPA of 3.39 out of 4.00 also has been awarded the Graded Autonomy by MHRD, Government of India. PDPU is one of the two private universities in India and the only University in Gujarat to receive this recognition in 2018. PDPU was established in 2007 by the Gujarat State Legislature Act. PDPU’s lush green campus is spread over 100 acres of land with state of the art academic and residential infrastructure of global standards. PDPU addresses the need for trained and specialized human resource for Industry worldwide. It intends to expand the opportunities for students and professionals to develop intellectual knowledge base with leadership skills to compete in the global arena. University aims to foster the spirit of global citizenship for all its stakeholders and create dynamic global culture on the campus.
