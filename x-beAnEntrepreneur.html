We select our incubatees via a screening process called I–Start.

It is a platform for students to submit their innovative Idea/Project. The program offers an opportunity to receive mentorship and funding for the development of their ideas. We at IIC, we select the project based on the idea, from the ideation level as well. The funding to convert your ideas into product is provided by IIC

Fill up an online form and submit. You can submit your form here.

Our initial screening committee would screen your idea based on your idea description in the form

Send in presentations in a format we provide you.

If we find your idea innovative enough based on the form, we shall call in for the written presentation based as per the format provided by us. The submitted presentations shall be screened by the experts of the field and selected projects shall enter the third & final round of screening.

walk-in for final round of presentations with your team.

Final selection shall happen based on your presentations and the final selection of candidate would be as per the committee’s voting.

A panel of experts would screen the presenting teams on following criteria:

  • Uniqueness/ Innovation
  • Feasibility
  • Market Assessment
  • Value to Society
  • Commercial Viability
  • Implementation
